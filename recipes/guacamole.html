Guacamole Recipe

Description

Now I will share you the recipe for making a Guacamole, which is an avocado-based dip or spread that originated in Mexico. So without going further deep into its origin and history, let us now start making this delicious avocado based dish.

Ingredients

  • 3 Avocados- peeled,pitted, and mashed
  • 1 lime, juiced
  • Salt - 1 tablespoon
  • Tomatoes, diced
  • Â½ cup diced onion
  • 3 tablespoons chopped fresh cilantro
  • 1 teaspoon minced garlic
  • 1 pinch ground cayenne pepper (Optional)

Steps to cook

  1. Mash avocados, lime juice, and salt together in a medium bowl; mix in tomatoes, onion, cilantro, and garlic. Stir in cayenne pepper.
  2. Serve immediately, or cover and refrigerate for 1 hour for improved flavor.
